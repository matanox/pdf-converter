Introduction
Around
70%
of
Europeans
aged
50
or
over
are

owners.
Home-ownership

high

This

It

Hence

acquired

what

have
prevented
others
from
doing

The

address
in

paper
is
how
older
Europeans
became
home-owners.
Did

markets
play
a
key
role,
or

from
family
the

behind

We

13

the
ﬁrst

the

ﬁrst-time

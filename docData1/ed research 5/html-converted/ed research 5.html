Fifth-grade

This

Day,

1983;
Brown

Smiley,
1977,
1978).
For
students
of lower
ability,
the
instruction should
probably provide
considera-
bly
more
practice
and feedback.
There
were two
components
to the
instruc-
tion in this
study:
recognizing
a text structure
and
using
a
text structure to
write
summaries.
Future research should

the distinc-

of
each
component.
Mean-

writing!)

classrooms.
